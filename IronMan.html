IRON MAN

Earth's mightiest hero

Here's a timeline of his life in the MCU:

  • 2008 Was attacked by terrorists in Afhganistan. After sustaining injuries from the attack he builds a mini reactor keep shrapnel from reaching his heart. The reactor also doubles as a power source for his armor.
  • 2010 His reactor is slowly killing him, but he soon watches video from his dad on how to fix it. War machine makes his debut.
  • 2012 Thor's stepbrother Loki invades Earth. In order to stop the threat a nuke is thrown into space by Tony.
  • 2013 Tony has PTSD so he challenges a fake terrorist. After dealing with him, he destorys all his suits.
    • 2015 Tony accidentally creates a malevolent AI.
  • 2016 Iron Man and Captain America get into an arguement over civil rights.
  • 2018 Tony attempts to stop Thanos from getting all of the infinity stones but fails.
  • 2019 Tony invents time travel and saves the universe, but at the cost of his own life.
